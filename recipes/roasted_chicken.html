How to Roast a Chicken

Learn how to make perfect roast chicken right in your own kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Dissolve ½ cup kosher salt (or ¼ cup table salt) in two quarts of water. Immerse the chicken completely in the solution and place in the refrigerator.
  2. You should let it soak for at least one hour, but no longer than five or six hours.
  3. Pour off the brine, rinse the chicken under cold running water, and pat it dry with paper towels. For extra-crispy skin, return the bird to the refrigerator and let it air-dry for another hour, or overnight, before roasting.
